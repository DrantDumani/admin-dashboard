13 Sentinels

I literally traveled to Jersey to buy this game. Almost made it late to work. And then I never opened the case. Despite the good story and strategy based gameplay. My brother never played it either. Wonder why.

Yakuza Kiwami

I restarted this game twice. And then I stopped playing right after the funeral both times. I wanted to stream it, but plans sort of fell through. Maybe I'll finally beat it this year. Not gonna try to tackle all of the sidequests though. Ah, who am I kidding. Of course I will. Once I have a long block of time to just sit down and relax with a game without any worries. Also, Kiryu is a good man.

Touhou Genso Wanderer

I started it, got overrun a few times before making it very far, and then kind of stopped playing. It's a good game and I should give it another try. Especially since the sequel is out. My bro beat the first boss, so it's not like it's impossible. He even made it up to the second boss! Maybe this shouldn't have been my first dungeon crawler game.

Xenoblade Chronicles

I own both this game and the 2nd one (with all of the DLC included!). They're so long, but I made the choice to purchase them. And the 3rd one comes out this year. Maybe I bit off more than I could chew. Wish I got to play the original back when I had way more free time. It's hard to not feel like you should be doing something else when you sit down to invest time into an 80 hour game.

Dillon's Dead Heat Breakers

The 3ds eshop is gonna close real soon. Get your act together and play this game so you can get to the rest of the 3ds backlog before it's too late! I shoulda played this instead of playing Splatoon 1 for 1300 hours! I'd give up more than half of those hours to play other games!
